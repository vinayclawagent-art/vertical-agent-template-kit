# Role Template Card: Resume Ops Agent

Source package: [[Vertical Agent Template Kit]]

## Buyer role
Job seeker / resume service operator

## Repeated job
Turn a messy resume + job description into a tailored, ATS-safe application packet.

## Agent promise
Produce a polished resume diff, cover note, and application checklist in under 10 minutes.

## Inputs
- Current resume
- Target job description
- Optional past wins / metrics

## Guardrails
- Never invent employment history.
- Mark every inferred claim as needs-user-confirmation.
- Preserve factual dates and company names.

## Review surface
- Before/after summary
- Highlighted risk claims
- Export checklist

## Launch wedge
Offer one paid “application packet refresh” before building accounts or a dashboard.
